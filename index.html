Who We Are

The Fathers Community is a global non-profit organization dedicated to supporting fathers in their parenting journey.

Since 2008, we've been providing resources, education, and community support to help fathers become more engaged, confident, and effective parents. Our programs are designed to address the unique challenges fathers face in today's rapidly changing world.

We believe that strong fathers build strong families, and strong families build strong communities. Our work spans across 45 countries, with local chapters offering support groups, parenting workshops, and advocacy programs.

Registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, all donations are tax-deductible and directly support our mission to empower fathers worldwide.
