Who We Are

The Fatherless Community is a global non-profit organization dedicated to supporting children growing up without fathers.

Since 2003, we've been providing mentorship, educational resources, and emotional support to help fatherless children thrive. Our programs address the unique challenges these children face, from emotional struggles to practical needs.

We believe every child deserves love, guidance, and support, regardless of their family situation. Our work spans across 60 countries, with local chapters offering mentorship programs, counseling services, and educational support.

Registered as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, all donations are tax-deductible and directly support our mission to empower fatherless children worldwide.
